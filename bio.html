My name is Jaiven Smith, I’ve lived in Lexington, Kentucky for my entire life. I currently attend the University of Kentucky as a major in Integrated Strategic Communication with a minor in Digital Studies. Learning these skills at a younger age has helped me to network with those in the local Lexington community and taught me the importance of polishing my professional appearance.

Through learning to polish my appearances, I’ve learned to creatively do the same for my clients. Check out my works page for some examples of my work.

I look forward to working together soon!
